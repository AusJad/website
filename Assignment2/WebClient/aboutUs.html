Welcome to the website for Murdoch Groceries - the on-campus place for all your grocery needs. Choose from our selection of fresh fruit and vegetables, our range of farm-fresh produce, and some of your favourite lollies. Since we're Guild owned and operated, all of our profits go straight back into student benefits. Guild members get a discount - just show your Student Card at checkout!

With our online ordering system, your groceries can be ready for you at the till when you arrive! Just sign the receipt, grab your groceries, and head home!

Based in Bush Court, our store is central to the whole campus, meaning no matter where you are, you're just a quick walk away!
